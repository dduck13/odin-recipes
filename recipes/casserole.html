Сottage Сheese Сasserole

Description

Instead of eating a bowl of cottage cheese, turn it into a creamy breakfast casserole. Make this easy cottage cheese casserole recipe and top it with fresh fruit for a fun new breakfast experience. (We have more topping ideas below!)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cottage cheese
  • 2/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl. Mix well.
  2. Pour the mixture into an 8x8-inch baking pan that's been lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake in a preheated 400-degree F oven for about 25 to 30 minutes or until light golden brown. Serve warm or cold.
